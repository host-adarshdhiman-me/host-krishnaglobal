Work Permit

A work permit visa is an official document that allows foreign nationals to legally work in a specific country for a designated period. This visa is typically issued based on employment offers and requires meeting certain criteria set by the host country's immigration authorities. It ensures that the visa holder complies with the country's labor regulations and contributes to the local workforce.

Business Visa
Explore More

Business Visa

A business visa is designed for individuals traveling to a foreign country for business-related activities. This visa allows for attending meetings, conferences, and negotiations, but not for long-term employment. It ensures compliance with the host country's regulations and supports international business relations.

Student Visa

Student Visa

A student visa is granted to individuals who wish to pursue education in a foreign country. This visa allows the holder to enroll in educational institutions, from schools to universities. It requires proof of admission and financial support, ensuring the student can cover tuition and living expenses during their stay.

Diplomatic Visa
Explore More

Diplomatic Visa

A diplomatic visa is issued to foreign diplomats and government officials for conducting official government business. This visa grants various privileges and immunities, ensuring the smooth performance of their duties without legal obstacles.

Residence Visa
Explore More

Residence Visa

A residence visa allows individuals to live in a foreign country for an extended period. It is often granted to those who have secured employment, family reunification, or other long-term stay reasons. This visa requires meeting specific criteria and provides a pathway to permanent residency.

Tourist Visa
Explore More

Tourist Visa

A tourist visa allows individuals to visit a foreign country for leisure, sightseeing, and recreational purposes. This visa typically permits short-term stays and requires proof of return travel and financial means to cover the trip's expenses.

PR Residence
Explore More

PR Residence

A PR Residence visa allows individuals to permanently reside in a foreign country. It is typically granted to those who have met specific criteria, such as having a job offer, family ties, or exceptional skills. This visa provides long-term residency rights and may lead to citizenship in the future.
